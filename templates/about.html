, Welcome to the Push-up Counter!

Overview

This web application uses a computer vision model developed with OpenCV and Mediapipe to count the number of push-up performed in a live video feed.

Using the Model

To count push-ups, the model uses your built-in webcam to track your body movement. To ensure accurracy, make sure that your whole body is in view while you are performing your push-ups. In case these setup requirements prevent you from using the model, you can watch a demonstration of the model here.

Push-up Counting and The Leaderboard

The Push-up Counter tracks how many total push-ups are associated with each account. Additionally, you can check out the leaderboard to see who has performed the most push-ups on this application! Both of these metrics can be found in the Dashboard.
