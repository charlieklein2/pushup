, Welcome to the Push-up Counter!

Overview

This web application uses a computer vision model developed with OpenCV and Mediapipe to count the number of push-up performed in a live video feed.

Using the Model

To count push-ups, the model uses your built-in webcam to track your body movement. To ensure accurracy, make sure that your whole body is in view while you are performing your push-ups.

Push-up Counting and The Leaderboard

The Push-up Counter tracks how many total push-ups are associated with each account. To view your push-up history, and a leaderboard of the users who've performed the most push-ups on this application, check out the Dashboard.
